About Me

Chi (Charles) Xu is currently a research assistant in Computing Science Department at Simon Fraser University, supervised by Prof. Jiangchuan Liu. He obtained his M.Sc. degree at Simon Fraser University in December, 2016. His current research interests include computer and network virtualization, performance issues in cloud computing, and hardware support for big data applications. He is actively seeking for full-time developer job starting from March, 2017.
